Big Bang Boxing

Welcome to Big Bang Boxing in Bundang, South Korea. Canadian Owned and operated!


Big Bang Boxing was established in xxxx and has a strong focus on intorducing newcomers to the sport of boxing along with developing professional boxers' careers.


Currently, we have several professional fighters training out of our gym, along with a multitude of younger and older students who want to learn how to box.






A Message from the Head Coach and Owner


Anyone can learn how to box. It's the best physical exercise that there is. No one wants to lift weights into oblivion, they want to have fun while working out! they want to train martial arts! They want to get in better shape! Here, we do all of these things! - Coach Joe



Coach Colin Joe Pineault has amassed an astounding undefeated record of 6 wins and 0 losses. His boxing ventures have taken him from cold Canada all the way to beautiful Korea!


Coach Joe has been boxing since 2008. After opening his own boxing gym in a foreign country, he focuses on bridging the gap between foreigners and Koreans through the art of boxing!
